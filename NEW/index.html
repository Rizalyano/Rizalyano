"Vision"

Dr. Jose P. Rizal Senior High School as a public educational institution envisioned its graduates to be responsible, industrious, zealous, active, loving, innovative, altruistic and nationalistic 21st century individuals whose skills, competencies and values are aligned to the vision, mission and core values of the Department of Education for the purpose of attaining educational objectives in the local and global community setting.

"Mission"

Dr. Jose P. Rizal Senior High School is committed to inculcate theoretical and practical knowledge with holistic academic and technical skills among its students while instilling love of God and country. Specifically, it commits itself to:

1. Mold students on the skills, competencies and values related to their field of specialization;

2. Deliver quality, accessible, inclusive and relevant education by constantly updating its curricular offerings responsive to the demands of the industry and the community;

3. Nurture competent, dedicated and passionate faculty members who will fully deliver the expected quality teaching and learning activities which is student centered;

4. Empower non – teaching personnel to provide quality and efficient services;

5. Provide technologically - equipped educational facilities aligned to the standards of the industry and the Department of Education; and,

6. Produce competitive graduates who possess skills, competencies and values that are responsive to the needs of the industry and the community both in the local and global community setting.

History

It started its operation in June 5, 2017 with two tracks offered namely: Academic and TVL. Under the Academic track, GAS and ABM. Under TVL track, CSS, CBF and EIM. It has a four – storey twelve (12) classrooms building, some of the classrooms are utilized as CBF laboratory, EIM laboratory, Computer laboratory, Science laboratory and Faculty room.

Dr. Jose P. Rizal Senior High School with School ID 342300 is located at Brgy. Sto. Cristo, DBB1, City of Dasmariñas, Cavite is one of the three (3) Stand-alone Senior High Schools in the City Schools Division of Dasmariñas operating since School Year 2017-2018. It was established by virtue of Resolution No. 102-S-2016 by the City Council on June 6, 2016.
